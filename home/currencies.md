# Currencies

At **conomy\_hq**, we offer support for various currencies to help you build flexible and scalable financial products. Our services allow you to work with both fiat and cryptocurrencies across different networks. Whether you’re handling transactions in local currency or crypto, we ensure compliance with the ISO 4217 standard for fiat currencies, and for cryptocurrencies, we use the format `token_network` (e.g., `USDC_SOLANA`).

If you need a currency that’s not listed, feel free to contact us, and we’ll explore adding it to our system.

### Supported Currencies

| USDC\_MATIC  | USD Coin on Polygon (MATIC)  |
