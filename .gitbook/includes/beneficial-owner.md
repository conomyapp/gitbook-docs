---
title: Beneficial owner
---


| Field | Type | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| name | `string` | Full name of the individual or the registered company name. |
| identificationNumber | `string` | National ID or passport number (individual) or tax ID (company). |
| ownership | `string` | Percentage of ownership or control in the company. |
| legalEntity | `boolean` | Indicates if the owner is a legal entity (company) or an individual. |
