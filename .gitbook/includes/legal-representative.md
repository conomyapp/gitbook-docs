---
title: Legal representative
---


| Field | Type | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| name | `string` | First name of the legal representative. |
| lastName | `string` | Last name of the legal representative. |
| email | `string` | Email address of the legal representative. |
| phone | `string` | Phone number of the legal representative. |
| powerOfAttorneyDocument | `string` | Base64-encoded document proving power of attorney. |
| pep | `boolean` | Whether the representative is a Politically Exposed Person (PEP). |
