---
title: account Info
---


| Field | Type | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| id | `string` | Unique identifier of the bank account. |
| accountNumber | `string` | Bank account number for identifying the account within the bank. |
| nickName | `string` | Nickname for the bank account. |
| country | `string` | Country where the bank account is held. |
| currency | `string` | Currency associated with the bank account. |
| financialInstitution | `string` | Bank associated with this account. |
